Build up a Portfolio

Your Task

Step 1 - Build up your speech.
  • Choose one of the issues studied in this unit: a) the role of editors, b) the importance of neutrality or c) the fight against disinformation.
  • Choose 3 documents. One we have studied in the classroom, one from this page and one from your own research. All three must illustrate the issue chosen.
  • Prepare your presentation: show how these documents relate to the topic you chose.
Step 2 - Make your presentation in front of two classmates.
  • Show your documents to your classmates and explain your point in about five minutes. Make sure you say how they illustrate the topic.
Step 3 - Answer your classmates' questions about your presentation.

Evaluation

Your oral performance will not be assessed. However, I will collect and mark the following:
  • Write a short presentation of your 3-document portfolio. This piece of writing will not be longer than 300 words. It will present the documents and explain how they illustrate the topic you have chosen.
  • The assessment will be based on the following:
    • You have presented the key elements of the documents.
    • You have shown how they relate to the issue.
    • Your have used suitable and relevant vocabulary.
    • You have communicated your ideas successfully.
