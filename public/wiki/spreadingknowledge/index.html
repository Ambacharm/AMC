Your task

Write the profile of an editor for Wikipedia's contributors' page.
Option 1: Choose Roman or Steven Pruitt.
Option 2: Imagine another editor.

A few tips:

Varie les adjectifs et nuance les avec des adverbes:

  • He's an incredibly driven man.
  • This is truly a mind-boggling feat.
  • Almost legendary status.

Utilise des subordonnées infinitives pour exprimer la visée:

  • The idea is to use it as a first point of research.
  • I'm very conscious of what it can mean to make knowledge free.

Donne des précisions à l'aide de subordonnées relatives:

  • ... experts who are usually paid to contribute and edit content.
  • ... the 19-year-old North Carolinian, whose alias is often shortened to AG.
