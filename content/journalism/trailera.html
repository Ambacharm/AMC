--- title: "Trailera" date: 2022-01-22T14:28:16+01:00 ---

The Watergate scandal started when the Nixon administration tried to cover up a break-in at the Democratic National Comittee's office in 1972. The robbery took place at the Watergate complex in Washington, D.C. The burglary and other illegal acts were exposed in investigations carried out by the media with the help of a mysterious informant, "Deep Throat". In the end, the scandal caused President Richard Nixon to resign.
